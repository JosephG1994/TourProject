Tours

Zootopia City Tour

Explore the central hub of Zootopia in our city bus tour. Experience the urban landscape of Savanna Central, with its high rises and thriving businesses. The City Tour includes stops at City Hall, the Natural History Museum, Zootopia Central Station, and Little Rodentia.

  • $120.00
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Ticket to Zootopia's Natural History Museum (admission not part of tour)

Zootopia Desert Tour

Experience the heat of Sahara Square, Zootopia's desert oasis. Experience a 5 day stay at the world famous Palm Hotel. Enjoy the following amenities included in the tour package:

  • Hotel room with spectacular views of Sahara Square
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • 5 dinner vouchers for a Palm Hotel restaurant of choice
  • World class casino
  • Grand pool complex
  • Private pool with cabana
  • Spa and fitness center
  • Palm nightclub and bar
  • Ticket to a Palm Hotel show

You also have the opportunity to explore the rest of Sahara Square in a self guided tour. Visit the various high-end shops in the district and landmarks like the Sunday Meerkat Market or the Mystic Spring Oasis.

  • $950.00
  • 5 Day, 4 Night Stay at the Palm Hotel

Zootopia Tundratown Tour

Enjoy the chill of Tundratown, the coolest place in Zootopia, in this skidoo shuttle tour and resort stay. Experience the year round arctic climate and winter living in Tundratown. Spend 2 days in Tundratown's Mountain Resort with the following amenities:

  • Hotel room
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • 1 day lift ticket with ski lesson
  • Equipment rental
  • Dinner in resort restaurant
  • 6 hour skidoo shuttle tour with dinner

The skidoo tour will take you around the urban and natural landscapes of Tundratown, making stops along the way. Ride the Flow Ped in Tundratown's Market Square and visit the various markets and shops in the area. Ice skate in Tundratown's famous skating rink. The skidoo tour will conclude with dinner at The Thaw, Tundratown's vibrant restaurant with live music and dancing.

  • $600.00
  • 2 Day, 1 Night Stay at Tundratown's Mountain Resort

Zootopia Rainforest Tour

Experience the natural beauty of Zootopia's lush and green Rainforest District in our famous riverboat tour. Enjoy spectacular views of Zootopia Falls. Take in the mountainous landscape filled with thick rainforest along with the treehouses and huts that lie within. View the Rainforest Gondolas, zepplins, and monorail from above our heads. Enjoy a full-course dinner while on board our riverboat.

  • $150.00
  • Duration: 6 hours
  • Dinner included
